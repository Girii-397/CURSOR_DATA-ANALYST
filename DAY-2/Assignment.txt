Probability :
The chance of something happening.
Experiment :
An action or process that produces results you can observe or measure.
Outcome :
The result of a single trial of an experiment.
Event :
A collection of one or more outcomes.
Permutation :
The arrangement of items where order matters.
Combination :
A selection of items where order does NOT matter.
Union :
The outcomes that are in either of two events (or both).
Intersection :
The outcomes that are in both events.
Complement :
The outcomes that are not in an event.
